Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This app collects minimal personal information and only uses necessary permissions to provide the app's features. Below is a summary of what is used and why.

Data Collection & Usage

  • Authentication: We use Firebase Authentication, Google Sign-In, and Facebook Login to help users create accounts and log in securely.
  • Location: We request access to your location to personalize the app experience. Your location data is not stored or shared with third parties.
  • Media Access: We may request access to your photos and media through the Photo Manager and Image Picker packages for profile image uploads or similar features.
  • Notifications: We use OneSignal and Firebase to send you important app updates or personalized notifications. You may opt out anytime from device settings.
  • In-App Purchases: We use RevenueCat and Stripe to manage subscriptions and payments. No payment details are stored directly by us.
  • Advertising: We use Google Mobile Ads SDK to show non-personalized ads. Your ad preferences can be managed through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

This app integrates third-party services that may collect limited data in accordance with their respective privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Core, Auth, Messaging)
  • Google Sign-In
  • Facebook Login
  • OneSignal
  • RevenueCat
  • Stripe
  • Google Mobile Ads

Your Control

You can choose to enable or disable permissions (like location or notifications) through your device's settings at any time.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as our features change. Future updates will be published here.
